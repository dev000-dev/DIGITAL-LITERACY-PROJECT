Responsible Social Media Use Checklist for College Students

DO's
- Think before you post – Ensure your content is appropriate and respectful.
- Protect your privacy – Use strong passwords and adjust privacy settings.
- Verify information before sharing – Avoid spreading misinformation.
- Maintain a professional image – Employers may view your profile.
- Respect others online – Be polite and avoid offensive comments.

DON'Ts
- Don't share personal or sensitive information – Avoid posting address, phone number, etc.
- Don't engage in online arguments or trolling – It can harm your reputation.
- Don't post inappropriate or offensive content – It may affect future opportunities.
- Don't believe everything you see online – Always fact-check information.
- Don't overuse social media – Maintain a balance with studies and real life.
